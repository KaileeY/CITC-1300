About Formula One

Formula One, or F1, is the highest class of single-seater auto racing, governed by the International Automobile Federation (FIA). The championship is known for its high-speed racing, cutting-edge technology, and races all around the world

What is Formula One?

  • The Sport: F1 features a series of races known as Grands Prix, held on both circuits and public roads around the world.
  • Teams and Drivers: The sport showcases the skills of the best drivers and the technological prowess of top teams like Ferrari, Mclaren, and Red Bull Racing.
  • Rules and Regulations: Governed by a complex set of rules designed by the FIA to ensure safety and fair competition, F1 is a blend of skill, strategy, and technology.
